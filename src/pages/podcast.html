SpotlightAIJA Podcast

Welcome to SpotlightAIJA, the podcast series presented by AIJA, the International Association of Young Lawyers.

The podcast offers practical tips to help young lawyers and more seasoned professionals improve their business and professional lives. In each season we talk to lawyers and other sector experts with valuable insights into the legal profession. From innovation, technology to leadership, marketing, creativity and branding, get the tools you need to succeed.

In the upcoming second season, profound hosts will discuss with key experts on current legal issues, sustainability, diversity and inclusion, business in Asia, legal tech and the future of the legal profession.
