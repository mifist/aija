Sponsorship committee

Our sponsorship committee is responsible for advising and making recommendations on the strategic direction and implementation of our annual sponsorship programme. It includes key AIJA members with a strong track record of fundraising for our events and the association. The committee is working with the AIJA Brussels team which is the primary contact point for all AIJA sponsorship activities.
