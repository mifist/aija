Antitrust

Our Commission addresses current antitrust and competition issues concerning businesses in various jurisdictions around the world. We offer our members an opportunity to get first-hand insights on the changes that are shaping the antitrust law today and will continue to guide its development in the future.

We work together with legal practitioners around the world to identify significant developments that the business needs to be aware of in areas such as mergers, litigations or cartels and anti-competitive agreements and practices.
