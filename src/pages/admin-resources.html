Resources

This section serves as a resource to members. Below you will find specific templates for letterheads, presentations, but also the membership brochure, the AIJA brand guidelines and assets, and information about how to organise seminars.

The brand assets may be used by AIJA members to identify themselves as members or refer to their AIJA membership or participation in AIJA activities. They may not be used together with any statement or material that may be harmful to the organisation, or in a way that implies formal affiliation with other organisations, unless prior written consent of AIJA. The request for permission can be submitted at office@aija.org. The AIJA logo must always be used in its entirety, without any changes made to its contents and shape.
